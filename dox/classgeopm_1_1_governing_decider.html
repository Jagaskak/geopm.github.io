Detailed Description

Simple implementation of a power governing leaf decider.

The governing decider uses the RAPL energy readings for each RAPL domain of control to distribute the given per-node power budget while staying under a given power cap. Each domain (ex: socket) subtracts the used DRAM power from the total power budget and gives the remainder to the package (cores).
